What is Consortium?

Consortium is a group project based task manager designed to help you keep on top of your project work. Be it for work, college or a side project, group projects can be hard to keep track of, so we created this web app to help you do just that.

Each project you create can be managed simply and efficiently with the help of Consortium. So why not give it a try today?

Tasks

Keep up to date with your upcoming deadlines to ensure you never fall behind.

Messenger

Chat with your team about your project, and track your progress.

Connect

Connect with your group members and work towards your goals.
